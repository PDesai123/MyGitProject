[3] Logon to GitHub

[4] In the upper-right corner of page, click your profile photo, then click Settings

[5] In the user settings sidebar, click SSH and GPG keys

[6] Click New SSH key or Add SSH key

[7] Enter a title for your ssh key 

[8] copy and paste your public key in the key field

[9] Click "Add SSH Key" button for complete process. 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
